The Best Movies According to Hannah

My top 3 Movies of all time

Spirited Away

There are many reasons why people should watch Ghibli movies.
The animation is beautiful. One gets an insight into a culture that presents somewhat different values than the western norms we grew up with. AND the music is beautiful

Back to the Future

This one is a personal favourite because it reminds me of happy moments in my childhood when my dad used to take me to Blockbusters. BUT there is also a reason why this movie is considered a cult classic: From a fun and engaging story, over iconic quotes to an amazing score.
I would recommened this to everyone who is bored on a normal night.

Pride and Prejudice

Given that Jane Austen is one of my favourite authors this version from 2005 cannot not be on this list.
Keira Kneightly and Matthew McFayden are very well cast, the love story is fun to watch even the 50th time and again the music is beautiful.
A must see for every fan of period dramas.
